CAREER OPPORTUNITIES

State Trooper

The Pandora State Patrol is the finest state law enforcement agency in the nation, providing the highest level of professional law enforcement services available. We hold both National and State law enforcement accreditation. Through a comprehensive education and enforcement plan, we promote safety throughout the State of Pandora.

The Pandora State Patrol actively enforces the State's criminal, traffic, motor vehicle, and driver's license laws. State Troopers are motivated, hard-working law enforcement officers who specialize in traffic safety. Our women and men patrol the State of Pandora using state-of-the-art training, equipment, technology, and resources to better serve the public.

We offer many exciting career and promotional opportunities. Troopers with two years of experience are eligible for specialty assignments such as Contraband Interdiction, Criminal Investigations, Dignitary Protection, DUI Patrol, Instructor, Motorcycle Patrol, Occupant Protection Specialist, Recruitment Officer, Pilot, Public Affairs, Special Response Team, K-9 Handler, and many more.

Troopers with five years of experience are eligible to promote to supervisory positions, opening additional opportunities for leadership and career advancement within the Pandora State Patrol.

Salary Information: $60,000 annually
